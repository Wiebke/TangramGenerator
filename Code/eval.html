Tangram is an old chinese puzzle, where 7 puzzle pieces called tans have to be placed within a given shape in a way such that the whole shape is covered and none of the pieces overlap. For a university project, I am attempting to randomly generate tangrams and automatically rank them according to how interesting they are. The ranking requires a measure of "interestingness" and this is where you come into play. In the following you will be presented with 6 shapes each and simply have to decide which of the shapes you find most interesting. Click on the arrow above (once it has completely filled) and decide which of the displayed shapes you find most interesting.

Above you can see 6 shapes. Choose the one that you find most interesting by clicking or tapping on it!

Thank you very much for your participation! If you want to, you can now go and try to solve some of these tangrams or rate some more tangrams of course.
